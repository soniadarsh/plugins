Plugin Name : LeadUp Pro WordPress plugin

Created by : https://www.inkthemes.com

Version: 1.0.7
 
Description : Lead Up allows the business owner to grab leads directly from their website so that all the leads can be utilised for the business purpose. You can directly import the leads through Lead Up option by uploading the CSV file. This will store all the leads on your Dashboard. Moreover you can put the lead up form in any page just by placing a shortcode on the page you desire to show the lead up form. Just place the following shortcode.

"[ink-leadcapture]"

This will set the form on the page. The leads will be stored on your dashboard.

You can create multiple fields in the lead up form as per your requirement. Yes, there are options given in the Custom Lead Form section, through which you can customize the lead up form as per your requirement. You can add checkboxes, radio buttons, text and text area. You can also adjust the mane of the fields as per your requirement.

If you want to display the form under the widget or sidebar then you can use the LeadsUp widget to do the same. You can drag and drop the widget and display it.

The plugin is simple to implement on the website. And it will work perfectly with any of the InkThemes.
